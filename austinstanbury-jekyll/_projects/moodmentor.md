Designed immersive tools for vocational and emotional skill-building for neurodivergent adults. Consulted with academic experts at UF Digital Worlds and UF Institute for Advanced Learning Technologies.

Deployed software across two health networks (Road to Responsibility, South Shore Support Services) serving thousands of individuals in Massachusetts.
