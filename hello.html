featured work

  • Virtual Pedagogical Agents

    $450K DoD/NSF-funded Mixed Reality and AI platform for microelectronics education. Published at CHI, CSCW, and in Computers and Education: AI.

  • PATHS Program

    NSF-funded 8-week semiconductor training program for autistic adults, combining VR training with industry mentorship from Micron, AMD, Intel, and GlobalFoundries.

  • MoodMentor

    VR mindfulness application for neurodivergent adults deployed across four Massachusetts health networks. Participants sustained focus for up to 10 minutes from a baseline of 1–2.

  • Bartow AR Murals

    $100K city collaboration — location-based AR experiences overlaid onto public murals with map-based navigation and local business integration.

art & exhibitions

  • Infinite Particularity

    Three-screen triptych testing whether constrained AI can develop authentic artistic perspective. Art Grant Recipient at MIT Reality Hack 2026. Featured at MiamiXR 2026.

  • MPulse

    Interactive climate installation with Kinect body tracking at ASU MIX Center. Community Impact Award at ASU Tech for Change: Remix the Future.

  • Shadows of Tomorrow

    Immersive XR climate narrative. Award of Excellence from GFAA. Art Grant Recipient at MIT Reality Hack 2025. Semifinalist in AWE XR Challenge. Published at ACM VRST '23.

  • Covid Reflections

    AR public health installation using AI body tracking to visualize COVID-19 symptoms. Deployed alongside UF Health mobile vaccination clinics. Published at ACM VRST '22.

Additional exhibitions at CICA Museum (South Korea), Alliance for the Arts (All Florida Exhibition), 4Most Gallery ("In The Machine"), and Gainesville Fine Arts Association. Collage work permanently installed at Connect Record Shop.
