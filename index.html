Austin Stanbury

Scholar and software developer working on practical applications of extended reality technologies. CEO and Co-Founder of Augmented Info Systems.

My work focuses on XR in classroom education, hospital settings, and neurodivergent support. Current projects explore AI pedagogical agents, mixed reality for microelectronics education, and contextual AI for personalized learning.

Research published in ACM venues including CHI, VRST, CSCW, and IEEE conferences.

The question that challenges me most: in a world filled with compelling computer augmentation, what is left of "the real"?
